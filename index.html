Bio

I am a postdoctoral fellow (InnoCORE AITA) at DGIST supervised by Prof. Daewon Seo (from Oct 2025), and completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at KAIST with Prof. Jaekyun Moon (Aug 2025). My research centers on communication-efficient federated learning and low-rank adaptation for foundation models, with broader interests across MARL, geometric DL, and evolutionary computation. I co-authored EvoFed (NeurIPS 2023) and am developing model-agnostic projection methods for message-efficient FL at scale.

Before this, I completed my undergraduate studies at the Tehran Polytechnic (Amirkabir University of Technology), where I was advised by Prof. MohammadAzam Khosravi and Prof. MohammadMehdi Ebadzadeh on RoboCup SSL platform and multi-agent learning. Between 2019 and 2020, I worked at BRTel Co. on Business Intelligence and BigData solutions for MCI Telecommunication.
